Need some help quitting social media?

You've come to the right place.

  1. Select an app you'd like to stop using
  2. Add the shortcut to your home screen:
    • On iOS: Open the link in Safari, click the Share icon, then "Add to Home Screen"
    • On Android: Open the link in Chrome, click "Add to Homescreen"
  3. Move or uninstall the real app, and replace it with the shortcut.
  4. Feel the warm embrace of real human interaction with your friends and family.
    • Or read a book, books are great.
